Samriddhi Pandey

Tasks: Hardware & Software, Research

Specific Tasks:

  • Organochlorine Pesticide (OCP) Filtration (Hardware): Worked on the end-to-end design of a localized OCP filtration system, covering both hardware and software components. Framed the contamination problem from a hydrodynamic and materials-compatibility perspective, addressing hydrophobic wall adsorption through geometry-controlled flow and a modular PETG-based filter architecture.   • Curli-Based Biofabric Printing (Hardware): Investigated curli nanofibres as programmable bioinks; assessed genetic tuning, extrusion, and adhesion for hybrid bioink–fabric systems.
