Education

Introduction

Synthetic biology sits at a crossroads of innovation and societal impact, and we believe it should not be developed in isolation. We strongly think that synthetic biology should extend beyond just the technicalities. We aim to explain complex biological technologies by making it more inclusive, as well as spread awareness about the effect of persistent OCPs and our solution to solve this issue. We have tailored our designs and methods to cater to a diverse range of audiences, including primary, secondary, high school students, colleges. Our efforts to make synthetic biology more accessible include a combination of interactive engagement, visual representations, and engaging educational resources. Rather than presenting it as a finished answer, we approached education as a way of inviting people to look inside the field.

Primary School

1. Presentation - Explaining our filter's concept using the cop catching the OCPs as an analogy, and a very simple brief on what biology is.

2. Worksheets - Colouring sheet and Maze to show where OCPs are used and where they can end up.

3. Visual representation - To help the students understand the concept of our filter, using plastic bottles, activated charcoal and food colouring to represent the OCPs.

Secondary school :

1. Presentation - Explaining what synthetic biology is and using the same cop analogy to explain our project.

2. Visual representation - To help the students understand the concept of our filter, using plastic bottles, activated charcoal and food colouring to represent the OCPs.

3. Skit - To show the impact of our project. Scenes showing unawareness, effects, etc.

High School/ Colleges:

1. Presentation - Explain what synthetic biology is and our project.

2. Games - Synbio Bingo

3. Discussion/ Workshop - Bioethics Courtroom, where synthetic biology is put on trial, and a “Would you sign this?” consent workshop.

3. Ideation Workshop

Local Language and Accessible Demonstrations:

1. SynBio Cookbook - Explaining synthetic biology as cooking.

2. Handbook - Showing how it would be with and without dry lab.

3. Make a video explaining how synbio is be used to make artificial flavour.

4. Collaborate with another iGem team and exchange our values on synbio.
