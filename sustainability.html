Sustainability

SDGs

  • Our project addresses the persistence of banned organochlorine pesticides in water bodies. By focusing on the removal of long-lasting chemical contaminants, we aim to contribute to improved water quality and safer water use for communities and agriculture.
  • Persistent pesticides like OCPs pose long-term health risks through contaminated water and food chains. By targeting environmental sources of exposure, our project supports approaches to promote public health and long-term well-being.
  • Our work highlights the lasting consequences of pesticide use in spite of bans. By addressing such issues, our project promotes responsibility in management of such chemicals and encourages sustainable approaches to environmental remediation.
  • Our project emphasises on interaction with diverse stakeholders, including farmers, communities, wastewater authorities, and academic experts. This encourages cooperation between communities, institutions, and experts to address shared challenges.
